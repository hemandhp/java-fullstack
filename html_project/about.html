Instructors

David Rodriguez

David is a seasoned hip hop choreographer with over 10 years of experience. He has worked with major artists and brings authentic street dance culture to every class. His teaching style emphasizes musicality, groove, and individual expression.

Emily Watson

Emily trained at the Royal Ballet School and brings classical technique to contemporary movement. Her classes blend precision with creativity, helping dancers develop both technical excellence and artistic expression.

Jordan Lee

Jordan specializes in contemporary and modern dance, with a focus on improvisation and somatic practices. They create a safe space for dancers to explore movement authentically and connect with their bodies.

Alex Thompson

Alex is a jazz and musical theater specialist who brings energy and showmanship to every class. With Broadway experience, they teach performance quality alongside technical skills.

Priya Patel

Priya fuses traditional Indian classical dance with contemporary styles, creating unique choreography that celebrates cultural diversity. Her classes explore rhythm, storytelling, and dynamic movement.

Marcus Johnson

Marcus is a breaking and street dance expert who competed internationally. He teaches foundational techniques while encouraging personal style development and creative battles.
